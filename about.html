About Kristin

Based in San Diego, CA, Kristin Plater (Play-ter) is a singer/songwriter/acoustic guitar player whose stunning voice has garnered comparisons to Pink, Sheryl Crow, Kelly Clarkson, and Patti Griffin.

Kristin was a finalist for the coveted Lilith Fair showcase in Detroit, the first place winner of the Michigan State University talent contest, a finalist in the Pantene Pro-Voice contest, and a runner up in Gibson's The Way I Am competition judged by Ingrid Michaelson.

From her album 24, Wonder Woman quickly made its way to the top of playlists on college radio stations across the nation. She is a rising YouTube sensation thanks to her song, Why Not, which inspires people worldwide to have fun all the time.

She has performed all over the United States as well as in Europe and Australia and has had the honor of singing the national anthem for the Detroit Red Wings and the Detroit Pistons.

Kristin writes acoustic pop with gripping songs about having fun, being in love, and finding a place in this world.
